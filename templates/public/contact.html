FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and major events, we recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance. For portrait sessions, 1-2 weeks notice is usually sufficient.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the remaining balance due on the day of the session or event.

How long until I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 1-2 weeks. Wedding and event photography takes 2-4 weeks for full editing.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! We cover events and sessions throughout Machakos County and beyond. Travel fees may apply for locations outside the town.
